ABOUT US

Harsha Vardhan Reddy One of the pioneers in the real-estate industry, has over 2 decades of expertise in banking and finance. He brings on-board a unique vision and management practice for his vast clientele to diversify and grow together. A foundation stands strong with a compatible team and an understanding leader, Harsha Vardhan being an exceptional leader formed a strong base team through the years. His expertise in the industry led to the inception of Vardhan Realty, Exclusive Real Estate Marketing & Sales Allied Services. It implements a customer-centric approach towards the real-estate market with a stronghold in Hyderabad, Bangalore, Mysore and Tirupati markets. Through years Vardhan Realty helped customers understand dream homes. He also established Mast Loans - An entity that provides optimum financial solutions for diverse requirements of the customers as well as the builders such as construction loans, developer finance, home loans, mortgage loans, transfer loans etc. From providing a new loan to assisting in transfer of existing loans to lower interest rates Mast loans helped shape the term financial consultation for all. Now with his proficiency and enriching industry knowledge he is instilling an organization named landowners Share Properties Pvt.Ltd. for the mutual benefit of all the sectors involved in the real-estate market.

Land Owners Share Properties Pvt.Ltd envisions to develop a collaborative harmony between the landowners-builders-customers. Enriching lives through the lens of spaces that create value to each of the sectors that are landowners , builders and customers is what we stand for. We epitomize the philosophy of understanding the perspective based lifestyle demands in this urban setup and deliver you with the space of your vision. Urbanization is ever-evolving and is in constant quest for spaces that can not only accommodate but also support you in your purpose-led life.
